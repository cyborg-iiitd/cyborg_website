    • About Us

    Our Goal

    We are the Robotics Club of the Indrapastha Institue of Information Technology, Delhi. Our work consists of creating both hardware as well as software solutions. We are focused on developing algorithms which derive new possibilities with a limited set of hardware in an adverse environment. Our club is based on the philosophy of Together Everyone Achieves More often abbreviated as TEAM. Through this club, we want to provide a space to all the interested people to come together and build things that matter. The club will help you with resources, space, funding and time.

    What We Do

    • Provide tech talks, guidance and mentorship from professors.
    • Provide technical help to students working on interesting projects.
    • Have a small lab with a large inventory for club members to work on their own projects.
    • Completely independent components issuing policy.
    • Provide funding / sponsorships for projects and components required for hackathons and other competitions.
    • Fortnightly workshops / discussions on key aspects of various streams of robotics and other problem statements.
    • Publishing design articles.
    • Regular discussions on Club forums, blog or group pages.
    • Float projects on real life problems and bring out a product or an idea to solve that.
