Projects

Maze Coverage and Basics of Graphs

Discussion on maze-solving, pathfinding algorithms. A head start for some important topics in CSE courses as well as MTH courses for freshers, especially for coding your own algorithm to make your bots work.

Basics of OpenCV

Basic introduction in the field of computer vision starting from basics of thresholding techniques and color sampling to things like histogram equalization, and further to some extremes such as detecting eyes and lips on a human face using haar cascades.

Introduction to Control Theory: PID Controller

An introduction to Control Theory and its numerous applications.The first session in series was on PID (proportional–integral–derivative) controls. Starting with a very simple model, we build up to real life applications like drone flying.
